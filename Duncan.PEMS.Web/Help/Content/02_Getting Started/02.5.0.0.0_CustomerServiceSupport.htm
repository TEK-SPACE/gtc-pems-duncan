Customer Service and Support

Technical Support is available directly through CivicSmart. If you are unsure of how to carry out a procedure or task after reading this user guide, please call the technical support line at:

 

United States Support

1-888-553-8622

support@civicsmart.com

 

When calling for technical support, please be located near a computer that has access to PEMS and have the following information available:

  • Your organisation’s name
  • Your question or a description of the difficulty you are experiencing

Please take note of any error codes or messages and any other information you think may be relevant, as this can assist technical support representatives in solving an issue in a timely manner.
