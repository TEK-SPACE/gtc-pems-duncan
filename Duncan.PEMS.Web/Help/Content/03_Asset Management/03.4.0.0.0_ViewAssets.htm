View Assets

From the asset inquiry results table you can select an individual asset and view its configuration and operational details in the View Asset screen. The information displayed for each asset will vary based on the type of asset (i.e. multi space meter, gateway, etc.).The View Asset screen may also link to other associated assets. For example, when viewing the details for a multi space meter, you can also view the spaces associated with that particular meter by clicking on the link to meter spaces.

You may also update or copy the configuration details of an asset from the View Asset screen, or reset a non-responsive asset deployed in the field.

 

To View Individual Asset Details

  1. Perform an asset inquiry.
  2. Click on the Asset ID in the Asset Inquiry Results table. The View Asset page displays all asset information, configuration detail and current functional status.
  3. Optional - Easily navigate to a different module by selecting the module from the drop down list in the lower right corner of the window and clicking on the button. The currently selected asset will be pre-populated in the new inquiry filter. The inquiry window will also include a button to allow you to return directly to the View Asset page.
  4. Click on the button to return to the inquiry results.

To Update an Asset

  1. Perform an asset inquiry.
  2. Select the asset you want to update by clicking on its Asset ID in the Asset Inquiry Results table.
  3. Click on the button.
  4. Make the necessary edits to the information on the Asset page.
  5. Click the button to return to the View Asset screen.

To Copy an Asset

  1. Perform an asset inquiry.
  2. Select the asset you want to copy by clicking on its Asset ID in the Asset Inquiry Results table.
  3. Click on the button in the View Asset screen.
  4. Modify the asset information and configuration detail fields.
  5. Click on the button.

To Reset an Asset

  1. Perform an asset inquiry.
  2. Click on the Asset ID in the Asset Inquiry Results table.
  3. Click on the button in the View Asset screen.
  4. PEMS will display a message confirming that the asset has been reset.
  5. Click on the button to return to the View Asset screen.
