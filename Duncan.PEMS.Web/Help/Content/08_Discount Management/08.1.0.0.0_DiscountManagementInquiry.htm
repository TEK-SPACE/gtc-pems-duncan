Discount Inquiry Filters

After selecting the Discount Management module in the Management Pane you may filter your discount inquiry using the following database fields:

Field Name Description
First Name, Last Name The first and last name of the person who applied for the discount.
Email Address Email address provided by the person who applied for the discount.
Account Status Specifies if the account is Active, suspended or terminated.
Discount Scheme Specifies the type of discount being used.
Discount Status Specifies if the discount is pending, approved or rejected.
Create Date Range Allows you to enter an account creating date rage.

 

Viewing the Discount Inquiry

After applying filters and refreshing the discount inquiry, the results will display in the table at the bottom of the screen.
