Add a Customer

Adding a customer to PEMS allows the customer to be selected after login.

To Add a Customer:

  1. Click on the button on the Customer Inquiry screen.
  2. Enter the customer identification information for the customer (all fields are required).
  3. Click on the button.
  4. Enter all of the required and\or available the customer identification information under each tab. Click on the button to save the settings.
  5. Click on the or buttons to return to the customer inquiry screen.
