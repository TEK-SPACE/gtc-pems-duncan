Roles

Roles manage the visibility of application features such as the ability to add or edit assets and users. Each customer can have their own unique roles in PEMS, which are managed in the Roles module.

 

To Perform a Role Inquiry:

  1. Click on Role Management in the management pane.
  2. Select a role from the Role Name drop down list.
  3. Click on the button to see the inquiry results.
     

 

The filter options are expanded by default. To collapse the filter fields so you can see more of the inquiry results on the screen click on the button.

 

To Add a New Role:

  1. Click on the button.
  2. Enter a name for the new role in the Role Name field.
  3. Check mark the box next to each right the new role will have (see right definitions below).
  4. Click on the button to create the new role.
     

 

To Edit a Role:

  1. Perform a role inquiry for the customer the role was created for.
  2. Click on the target role in the role inquiry screen.
  3. Click on the button.
  4. Edit the role as necessary. (not working)
