Client Management

The Client Management module allows you to inquire into existing customers and add new customers into PEMS.

 

To access the Client Management module:

Click on Client Management in the management pane.

 

     

 

To perform a customer inquiry:

  1. Enter the customer name or customer ID in the inquiry screen.
  2. Click on the button to see the inquiry results.
     

 

The filter options are expanded by default. To collapse the filter fields so you can see more of the inquiry results on the screen click on the button.
The customer name or ID must match how the customer was set up in PEMS (not case sensitive) in order to get inquiry results. If you leave the Customer Name and Customer ID fields blank the inquiry will return all customers in PEMS.

 

To Add a Customer:

  1. Click on the button on the Customer Inquiry screen.
  2. Enter the customer identification information for the customer (all fields are required).
  3. Click on the button.
  4. Enter all of the required and\or available the customer identification information under each tab. Click on the button to save the settings.
  5. Click on the or buttons to return to the customer inquiry screen.

 

     

 

To Update a Customer:

  1. Click on the customer ID in the customer inquiry results table.
  2. Update the appropriate fields for the customer in the Assets screen. Click on the button to save the settings.
  3. Click on the or buttons to return to the customer inquiry screen.
