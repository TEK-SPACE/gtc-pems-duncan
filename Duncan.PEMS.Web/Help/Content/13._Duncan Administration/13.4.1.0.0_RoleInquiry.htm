Role Inquiry

A role inquiry will show you what rolls have been created for each customer in PEMS.

To Perform a Role Inquiry:

  1. Select a customer from the Customer Namedrop down list.
  2. Select a role from the Role Name drop down list.
  3. Click on the button to see the inquiry results.
     

 

The filter options are expanded by default. To collapse the filter fields so you can see more of the inquiry results on the screen click on the button.
